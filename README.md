# MindBloom-
A whimsical pixel adventure where every puzzle grows your world.
Story

The Kingdom of Numbers has lost its magic.
Each forest, village, castle and temple has become frozen because the "Logic Crystals" disappeared.
The player is the newest Puzzle Keeper.
Every completed puzzle restores color and life to the kingdom.

Instead of simply solving Sudoku boards, every puzzle unlocks:
new areas
new NPCs
collectibles
outfits
pets
music
